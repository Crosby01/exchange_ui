Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about using ExChange

What is ExChange and how does it work?

ExChange is a platform that connects users who need to exchange PayPal funds with verified exchangers. Our platform vets all exchangers to ensure they are legitimate and trustworthy. Users can browse available exchangers, view their profiles, ratings, and services offered, then contact them directly to arrange an exchange.

Is it safe to exchange funds through this platform?

Yes, ExChange prioritizes safety and security. All exchangers on our platform go through a rigorous verification process, including identity verification, business verification, and PayPal account verification. Additionally, our rating and review system helps users make informed decisions by seeing others' experiences with each exchanger.

However, we recommend users to:

  • Only work with verified exchangers on our platform
  • Check reviews and ratings before proceeding
  • Start with smaller transactions with new exchangers
  • Report any suspicious behavior immediately
How do I find the right exchanger for my needs?

To find the right exchanger:

  1. Use our search and filter options to narrow down exchangers based on the services you need (PayPal to Bank, PayPal to Crypto, etc.)
  2. Review their profiles, including their verification status, success rate, and volume of transactions
  3. Check their ratings and read reviews from other users
  4. Consider their response time and availability
  5. Contact them directly through the platform to discuss your specific needs and their rates
What fees does ExChange charge?

ExChange does not charge any fees to users looking to exchange funds. The platform is free to use for finding and contacting exchangers. Exchangers may charge their own fees for their services, which will vary depending on the type of exchange, amount, and current market rates.

For exchangers, there is a monthly subscription fee to maintain verified status on our platform. This helps us ensure that only serious and professional exchangers are available to our users.

What should I do if I encounter an issue with an exchanger?

If you encounter any issues with an exchanger, please:

  1. First, try to resolve the issue directly with the exchanger through their contact channels
  2. If the issue persists, use our "Report an Issue" option in the support section
  3. Provide all relevant details, including transaction information, communication history, and any evidence
  4. Our support team will investigate the matter and take appropriate action
  5. For serious issues involving fraud, we recommend also reporting to the relevant financial authorities

We take all reports seriously and will suspend or remove exchangers who violate our terms of service or engage in fraudulent activities.
